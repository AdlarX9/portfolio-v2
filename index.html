I received my first 3D printer in 2018 and I've been learning how to print whatever I wanted to with it. I got since there a second one and a resin printer, always searching to improve my creations.

I have now created a lot of 3D printed figurines and cosplay props for my friends and me. Some of them required me a lot of time and accumulate hundreds of hours of printing.
